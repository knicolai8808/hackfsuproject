Meet THE BOT



This year's robot has a 6 motor lift with a 7:1 gear ratio for being able to lift objects and hang without burning out. It has a 2 motor claw with a 7:1 gear ratio to be able to handle a 6 to 8 star capacity and 2 cube capacity. Our robot has a 4 motor speed geared drive for quick maneuvering and a reverse bulldog for stability. We are able to consistently high hang at the end of matches. We are currently working on a 10 pt autonomous with a backup 5 pt autonomous program.

Meet The Coach

Mr. Hall
Head of Program


Dominique Hall is the robotics teacher and coach for Winter Haven High School's VEX Robotics competition team. He has been with the robotics program for the last 5 years and has been a very inspirational coach to the students. Mr. Hall is never gives the students the answers to their problems or questions, but rather asks them questions to help get them thinking so they can find the solution themselves. As his students have noted, Mr. Hall has been both an amazing mentor and role model to all of his students. He is very passionate about education and wants to see every student succeed.
