2016 - 2017 | Starstruck


This year's robot has a 6 motor lift with a 7:1 gear ratio for being able to lift objects and hang without burning out. It has a 2 motor claw with a 7:1 gear ratio to be able to handle a 6 to 8 star capacity and 2 cube capacity. Our robot has a 4 motor speed geared drive for quick maneuvering and a reverse bulldog for stability. We are able to consistently high hang at the end of matches. We are currently working on a 10 pt autonomous with a backup 5 pt autonomous program.

2015 - 2016 | Nothing But Net


Team #5245D was an all girls team led by Kristianna. They had a double shooter flywheel design with high accuracy. They had a good season and competed well in the Tampa League and at VEX state.

2014 - 2015 | Skyrise


This was the last year of team #5245. This season was seniors Allan, Andrew, and Joseph's last year competing. We had a solid robot design with few flaws. We competed well throughtout the season, winning Tournamnet Champion at the Tampa League and the Design award at the Lakewood Ranch Tournament, qualifying us for VEX State. We competed well at VEX state and ranked high. Our design was a double four bar lift with a triange tread intake.

2013 - 2014 | Toss Up


This was the year of our glory. Without previous experience by competing in local competitions, team #5245 caome to the FL TSA State VEX competition and did very well. They were very well rounded in all the aspects of the competition and ended up leaving with the 1st place VEX Excellence Trophy. This qualified them for both the Florida VEX State Competition anf the TSA National VEX Competition. We ranked in the top 10 in both competitons.

2012 - 2013 | Sack Attack


This year was our very first year ever competing in robotics. We had no clue what we were doing and were completely crushed by our opponents. However, this was a good learning experience as we were able to talk to other teams and learn about what they do and how they do it. We were very proud of what we were able to accomplish that year with our little knowledge and resources.
